German Language Courses

All courses are intensive, 12-week programs aligned to official Goethe-Institut exam preparation. Classes are small (max 8 students) for personalized feedback and rapid progression.

A1

Absolute Beginner

Perfect if you've never studied German before. Learn survival phrases, basic grammar, and everyday vocabulary.

  • Alphabet & pronunciation
  • Simple greetings & introductions
  • Present tense fundamentals
  • Elementary written tasks
Enroll
A2

Elementary

Build on basic skills. Handle familiar topics, past tense, and simple conversations about daily life.

  • Past & present tenses
  • Narrative skills
  • Restaurant, shopping, hotel German
  • Email & short text writing
Enroll
B1

Intermediate

Speak with more fluency. Discuss ideas, past experiences, and handle more complex social situations.

  • Complex tenses & modes
  • Opinion & argument skills
  • Professional communication
  • Formal letter writing
Enroll
B2

Upper Intermediate

Advanced fluency. Discuss abstract ideas, literature, current events. Qualify for German universities.

  • University readiness
  • Abstract language
  • Media comprehension
  • Academic writing
Enroll
C1

Advanced

Near-native proficiency. Master idioms, nuance, and sophisticated written & spoken German.

  • Cultural sophistication
  • Literary analysis
  • Professional mastery
  • Career advancement

Course Structure & Progression

Level Duration Weekly Hours Certification Next Step
A1 12 weeks 20-25 hours Goethe A1 → A2
A2 12 weeks 20-25 hours Goethe A2 → B1
B1 12 weeks 20-25 hours Goethe B1 → B2 or Career
B2 12 weeks 20-25 hours Goethe B2 German Universities
C1 12 weeks 20-25 hours Goethe C1 Master's Programs

Course Format

  • ✓ Class Size: Maximum 8 students for personalized feedback
  • ✓ Schedule: 3–4 sessions per week, morning/evening options
  • ✓ Format: Interactive, conversation-focused with grammar scaffolding
  • ✓ Materials: Official Goethe course books + WLS supplementary resources
  • ✓ Exam Prep: Mock exams every 2 weeks with detailed feedback
